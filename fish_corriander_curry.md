# Fish and corriander curry

## Sauce
- 1 onion (used white, recipe says red)
- 1 garlic clove
- 2cm ginger
- 1 red chilli (fresh)
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1cm coconut block
- 1/2 chicken stock
- Squirt tomato paste
- 300ml water
- Bunch corriander

## Meat
- White fish

## Veg
- Used brocolli, carrots
- Recipe says spinach

- Chickpeas
