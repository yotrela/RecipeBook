# Gazpacho Soup

- 500g Tomatoes
- garlic clove (take middle out)
- 1/2 small red onion
- 1/2 cucumber
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp sherry vinegar
- 1 pepper (we used red, recipe says green)
- 1tsp Salt

## Extras
- 1/4 Lemon
- Soy sauce
